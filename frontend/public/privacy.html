Introduction

InstaPay (“we”, “us”, or “our”) provides payment links, auction-style listings, and digital delivery services. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use our services.

Information We Collect

  • Information you provide, such as name, email address, product details, or bid amounts.
  • Transaction-related data from our payment processor (e.g., Stripe), such as payment status and identifiers.
  • Log and device data such as IP address, browser, pages viewed, and timestamps.
  • Files you upload for digital delivery (stored securely for fulfillment).

How We Use Information

  • To create and manage payment links and auction listings.
  • To process payments and deliver digital downloads after purchase.
  • To send transactional communications (e.g., purchase confirmations, auction winner notifications).
  • To protect against fraud, abuse, or violations of our policies.
  • To improve and maintain our services.

Sharing of Information

  • Payment Processing: We share relevant data with Stripe to process transactions.
  • Email Delivery: We use email providers to send transactional messages.
  • Storage: Digital files may be stored with cloud storage providers for secure delivery.
  • Service Providers: We work with vendors who assist in operating our services under appropriate safeguards.
  • Legal: We may disclose information to comply with law, protect rights, or respond to lawful requests.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, information may be transferred.

Data Retention

We retain information as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Your Choices

  • You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information where applicable.
  • You can opt out of non-essential communications. Transactional messages may still be sent when necessary.

Security

We implement technical and organizational measures designed to protect your information. No system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

International Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your own. We take steps to ensure appropriate protections are in place.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this Policy
